Connect 5 is a training programme that gives participants the skills and confidence to have enabling conversations with people in their work and lives. Informed by contemporary evidence, these conversations empower individuals to take action that improves mental health and wellbeing. Connect 5 is now delivered by Solent Mind, Thameside Oldham and Glossop Mind, Medway Council, Havent and East Hants Mind, Healthy Cornwall, Swindon Borough Council, Action Together, Stockport and District Mind, Cumbria Learning and Improvement Collective and Greater Manchester commissioned by Manchester Health and Care Partnership.
